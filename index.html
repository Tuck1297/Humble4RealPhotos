About Me

    Hey, I'm Tucker and I am the photographer 
    behind all the content you see on this 
    website. Pretty neat, right? 
    
    You may ask what motivated me to dive into 
    photography and my answer for you is nature. 
    I have always enjoyed the outdoors from a 
    young age and I eventually discovered that 
    some of what I saw could not be put into 
    words. As a result, I picked up a camera 
    and the rest is history. 
    
    Lately, I am focusing my photograph 
    creativity in exploring the obvious and 
    often overlooked aspects of local, state, 
    and national parks. Especially, the night 
    sky and how it can transform an entire 
    landscape. So, come and join me in 
    exploring this world of ours by following 
    me on instagram.
